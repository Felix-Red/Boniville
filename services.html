OUR SERVICES

UNLIMITED WIFI

Experience fast, reliable, and secure Wi-Fi connectivity throughout Boniville Student Residence. Stay connected for online classes, streaming, and social activities with seamless internet access across all your devices.

BACKUP POWER

Never worry about power outages! Our backup power system ensures continuous electricity for your essential needs, keeping you connected, productive, and comfortable at all times.

DAILY TRANSPORT

Enjoy safe, reliable, and convenient transport to and from campus. Our scheduled shuttles ensure you get where you need to be, on time and stress-free.

CLEANING OF COMMUNAL SPACES

We ensure clean, hygienic, and well-maintained communal spaces for your comfort. Our dedicated team keeps lounges, kitchens, and shared areas spotless, creating a healthy living environment for all residents.

LAUNDRY SERVICE

Enjoy the convenience of our on-site laundry facilities, equipped with modern washers and dryers. We make it easy to keep your clothes clean and fresh without leaving the residence.

SECURITY

Your safety is our priority. With 24/7 surveillance systems, we ensure a secure and peaceful living environment for all residents.
