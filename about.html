THE FIELDS

We’ve been providing inspired spaces where people can live life to the fullest for over a decade. Backed by the expertise of City Property, with over 50 years of property management experience, our success lies in the dedicated on-site management and maintenance teams that take care of our community and properties.

We firmly believe in our vision of urban rejuvenation, creating an environment where our students can live, work, and play in a safe environment. It’s part of our goal to create a neighbourhood that will make our capital city proud and showcase this dynamic community to the rest of the country. As a tribute to the area, two sculptures by well-known sculptor Marco Cianfenelli were commissioned by City Property.

You're in Great hands

We know it takes more than just a building to make a community. That’s why we’re so passionate about creating inspired living spaces that suit your lifestyle. Whether you’re a student or a working professional, our hands-on approach, quick response time, and on-site team ensure that we’re always there for you.
