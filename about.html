About Us

Welcome to Boniville Student Residence—a modern, welcoming, and vibrant living space designed exclusively for students. At Boniville, we understand that student life is about more than just academics; it’s about creating a home where you can thrive, grow, and build lifelong connections.
Our residence provides a convenient and comfortable environment that perfectly balances studying, socializing, and relaxation. With fully furnished rooms, high-speed internet, and thoughtfully designed communal study areas, we empower you to focus on your academic success in a space built for productivity.

Safety and support are at the heart of what we do. With 24/7 security and on-site support, we ensure a safe, stress-free experience for all our residents. At Boniville, you can rest assured that your well-being is our top priority, allowing you to concentrate on achieving your goals while enjoying every aspect of student life.
Boniville Student Residence—where comfort, connection, and growth come together to create your ideal home away from home.

Our Vision

To be the leading student accommodation provider that fosters academic success, personal growth, and community connection, creating a positive and inclusive environment for students from all walks of life.

Our Mission

To provide safe, comfortable, and affordable student housing with exceptional facilities that promote learning, growth, and well-being. We are committed to:

  • Offering high-quality, fully-furnished living spaces.
  • Building a supportive community where students feel valued and connected.
  • Ensuring safety and security as top priorities.
  • Creating an environment that balances study, social activities, and relaxation.

Why Choose Boniville?

  • A safe, vibrant, and inspiring environment tailored for students.
  • Modern, fully-equipped spaces for studying, relaxing, and connecting.
  • A community-driven experience that ensures a home away from home.
  • Proximity to essential services and educational institutions.

Our Promise

At Boniville, we don’t just offer a place to stay we offer a place where memories are made, where learning is nurtured, and where every student feels like they belong.
