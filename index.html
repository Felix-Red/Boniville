About Boniville

Boniville Student Residence offers a modern and welcoming living space tailored to meet the needs of students. It provides a convenient and comfortable environment for studying and socializing. Boniville features fully furnished rooms, high-speed internet, communal study areas, and recreational spaces to foster a sense of community. With round-the-clock security and on-site support, Boniville ensures a safe and stress-free experience, allowing students to focus on their academic and personal growth.
